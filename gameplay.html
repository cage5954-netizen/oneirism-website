Gameplay Overview

Oneirism is a single-player traversal game with a linear progression structure. Players must navigate a sequence of surreal obstacle arenas, platforming challenges, and cinematic trap sequences. There is no combat â€” the core gameplay loop is environmental mastery.

Level Progression

1. Spawn & Obstacle Arena

The player spawns at a glowing rune, then learns traversal by navigating molten spikes and a sweeping sword trap.

2. Chain Bridge & Blades

A suspended golden chain bridge is crossed while dodging giant spinning blades. This tests rhythm and positioning.

3. Parkour Descent & Vertical Ascent

Players drop into a black void with floating monoliths, pillars, and platforms, making full use of vertical climbing and motion matching.

4. Lightning Axe Sequence

Stepping forward triggers a cinematic trap: a lightning axe rises, spirals, then crashes toward the player. Time to run.

5. Meme Room & Final Ascent

A hidden chair easter egg references Vergil from Devil May Cry. From there, players ascend glowing cubes through an anti-gravity final climb to reach the summit.
