The Gameplay of Oneirism

There are no enemies. No health bars. Just you, a surreal traversal gauntlet, and a level that feels — for better or worse — alive. Oneirism isn’t about fighting. It’s about understanding a space that changes as you move through it.

Spawn & Core Traversal

You awaken by a glowing rune. There’s no tutorial — but you’ll figure it out.

Weapon Slope & Rotating Blades

The climb begins. A slope littered with archaic blades forces you to time each dash. Miss a beat? Back to the rune. Death here is educational.

The Golden Chain Bridge

A narrow chain bridge suspended over lava and flanked by rotating scythes. It's the midpoint — visually grand, mechanically unforgiving.

Vertical Parkour Pillars

The level pivots to verticality. Using UE5’s mantle system, you climb ancient monoliths — no room for error, and absolutely no handrails.

Lightning Axe Event

Triggered by proximity, a suspended axe ascends, spins, and then locks on to your location.

Comedic Interlude

You escaped — barely. Now you’re in a dim room with a plastic chair and a katana. An homage to Devil May Cry? Maybe. A palette cleanser? Definitely.

Anti-Gravity Zone

The world changes. You’re no longer in lava-land — it’s metal, glowing, and strange. The low-gravity puzzle tests your air control, as you leap onto floating blue cubes toward a summit in the stars.

Final Summit

You arrive — A glowing rune, but will it answer you?
