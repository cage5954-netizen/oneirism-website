THE WANDERERS

Their roles are unclear.

But they’re not gone.

The Sleeper

You play as a nameless dreamer caught in looping states. Agile, silent, uncertain. Every move is a guess. Every guess feels like memory.

The Fireman

An ancient sigil that wakes and resets you. Is it helping? Watching? Judging? No one knows—but it glows, and you obey.

The Forger

It sees you. It doesn’t act, but it lingers. Its form is unclear, but its gaze is always exactly where you are.
