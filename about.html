About Oneirism

Oneirism is a surrealist traversal game built in Unreal Engine 5. It blends dream logic with environmental storytelling, platforming, and atmospheric design. Set in a lava-drenched voidscape, players explore a linear progression of cinematic arenas and mechanics — all designed to challenge intuition rather than just reflex.

Development Story

This project was independently created and implemented by John Zhang, integrating skills in level design, animation systems (UE5’s motion matching), and game logic. Oneirism began as a personal exploration of narrative-ambient design, then evolved into a full prototype reflecting themes of perception, liminality, and surreal traversal.

Design Principles

  • 🎮 Movement-Centric: No combat — only traversal, timing, and situational awareness.
  • 🎭 Story Through Space: Environments themselves communicate tone and progression.
  • ⚙️ UE5 Technologies: Utilizes Niagara effects, Blueprint scripting, cinematic triggers, and motion matching locomotion.
  • 🧠 Built with Design Thinking: Iterative level construction based on playtesting and thematic refinement.
